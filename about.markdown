Currently, I am mostly working on microbes related to the [Black Soldier Fly (*Hermetia illucens*)](tklammsteiner.github.io/fromwastetofeed) and finding ways to improve  insect rearing	in the context of a circular economy. My interests include data science and visualization, microbiome research, and making thinks efficient in a sustainable way.
